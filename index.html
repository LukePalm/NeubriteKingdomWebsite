Neubrite Kingdom

Neubrite Kingdom is a Vanilla Minecraft server that has been around since 2014! We offer a unique community survival experience to players from all over the world. We are currently looking for new players to join and become the next generation of members! Click below to apply.

About The Server

We are a current release server (no snapshot) that includes a few plugins to protect against griefing.
